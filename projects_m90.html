Solid Modeling M90

Project Information

Year: Fall 2019
Course: Computer-Aided Design
Time: 3 weeks

Task

The project requires that you use an existing product as the basis for creating a solid model, document your process thoroughly, and then use the experience to suggest ways in which both the modeling process and the product itself could be improved.
